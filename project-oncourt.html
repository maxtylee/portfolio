Wrist-mounted motion tracker with real-time feedback for tennis athletes.

Abstract

OnCourt is a wearable wrist-tracker designed to analyze tennis forehand swings. It uses an MPU6050 sensor and ESP32 microcontroller with an OLED screen to record and display movement data. The device visualizes motion curves and highlights inconsistencies in swings, helping players improve their technique.
