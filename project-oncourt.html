OnCourt: Athletic Training Tracker

Mechanical Design, Prototyping & Testing

Demo of wrist swing tracking using MPU6050

Overview

We designed a wearable tennis tracker to monitor wrist motion and evaluate the swing technique of players in real-time. This included the design of a lightweight casing for ESP32, MPU6050, and OLED display, along with wrist strap integration. Prototypes were tested against amateur and experienced players to provide actionable biomechanical feedback.
